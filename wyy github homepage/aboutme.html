Shanghai

Hi, I am Isabel. I was born in Shanghai, China, an international metropolis. It combines both Chinese traditional charateristics and Western style in many aspects, such as politics, economy, culture and architecture. During 19th-20th century, there are French and British concession established in Shanghai. The best example is the Bund. The architecture is more like European style.
In recent years, many skyscrapers have been built in the Pudong District, the other side of Huangpu River, making Shanghai a significant financial, trade and shipping center in the world.

Hangzhou

If Shanghai is like New York in the United States, Hangzhou must be like Boston. Every summer, I will take a trip to Hangzhou, boating in the West Lake to view the beautiful scenery. It's my favorite city, with a history of more than 2,000 years.
